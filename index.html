CRYPTO KRAKEN - AN OVERVIEW

CryptoKraken focuses on creating an ecosystem for NFT creators, collectors & enthusiasts in the NFT space, that provides services like minting, creating generative art for your NFT Collection and also special features and utilities for the owners of our NFT projects.

We also focus on creating NFT Projects of our own, currently 2 projects are live on Opensea, PolyMonkeys & MicroPuppiesNFT. An exclusive feature is that - both our collections are INTER-BREEDABLE!! An individual who owns atleast one NFT from both the collections are eligible to access this feature. These newly bred NFTs form a new exclusive collection called BOBBLE MUTANTS.

And all our NFTs are based on Polygon because why not 'Polygon' as it uses the same Ethereum you love and is gas free!! Feel free to follow our twitter and Medium for more info.
